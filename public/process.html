Our Process

A proven, repeatable system for filling gyms. Here's exactly how we work.

1

Discovery Call

We hop on a 30-minute call to understand your gym, your market, your current marketing, and your goals. No cookie-cutter plans — everything starts with listening.

2

Strategy & Roadmap

Our team audits your existing presence and builds a tailored marketing roadmap. We identify the biggest levers — whether that's ads, SEO, retention, or all three.

3

Launch & Execute

We build the assets, launch the campaigns, and start driving leads. You'll see results within the first 2–4 weeks. We handle everything — you focus on training members.

4

Optimize & Scale

Marketing isn't set-and-forget. We continuously test, tweak, and scale what's working. Monthly reporting keeps you in the loop with full transparency.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long before I see results?

Most gyms see their first leads within 1–2 weeks of launching ads. SEO and content strategies take 2–3 months to build momentum but deliver compounding returns.

Do I need a big budget?

Not at all. We work with gyms spending as little as $500/month on ads. We'll recommend a budget based on your market and goals.

What if I already have a marketing person?

Great — we can work alongside your team or handle specific channels. Many of our clients use us to supplement their in-house efforts.

Is there a long-term contract?

We offer month-to-month agreements after an initial 3-month commitment. We believe in earning your business every month.
